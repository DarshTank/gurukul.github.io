Who We Are ?



















College Principal is very particular in appointing the lecturers. This is the reason nobody takes tuition classes after the college hours like students of other colleges. Our faculties present educational process/ programs in a congenial manner. My college charges nominal fees as compared to other colleges in the city due to which students from all strata of society are able to study here.
